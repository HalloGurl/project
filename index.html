Genshin Summarize

Gensin Imapact is an online role playing game based on a lore enriched story.The story revolves around the protagonist who is in search of their sibling. Throught their journey the protagonist travels through seven different nations. As a result he comes into contact with the archons of the seven nations and is always willing to help them out. In their journey the protaganist is accompanied by Paimon who acts as his guide through the nation of Teyvet.

Region


Mondstadt – The City of Freedom is the player’s first brush with the world of Teyvat. It is governed by Lord Barbatos, the Anemo Archon. However, Barbatos’ presence is rarely felt among the people of Mondstadt as he refuses to rule over his people directly. Though it may be strange to those from other nations, it rings true to the region’s ideal of freedom. Mondstadt of Genshin Impact is inspired by the real-world country of Germany.



Liyue – Liyue values the sanctity of contracts above all else. Thus, the people gather yearly during the Rite of Descension to heed the guidance of their Geo Archon, Morax. As one of the oldest Archons, Morax is a wise god, providing counsel to ensure prosperous trade. However, as the Genshin Impact Archon Quest reveals, he’s growing weary of his prominent role. Liyue is based off real-world China.



Inazuma is the Land of Eternity and the most hostile region that players have encountered thus far. Presided over by the cut-throat Electro Archon, Raiden Ei, locals run the risk of losing their Genshin Impact Vision due to the Vision Hunt Decree. Taking these Visions away brings Inazuma one step closer to eternity. Inazuma takes inspiration from real-world Japan.



Sumeru – As of writing, Sumeru (as well as the following regions) has yet to be released. As such, players don’t have much to go on yet. What the community does know is that Sumeru prizes the virtue of wisdom and is presided over by the Dendro Archon, Kusanali. Sumeru Academia can be found here, and it’s one of the most prominent educational institutions in Teyvat. Notably, Genshin Impact’s Lisa was once a student there. Sumeru is reportedly based on real-world SWANA regions.


Fontaine – NPCs from Fontaine suggest that the region prizes technology, culture, and arts. The toymaker Bertrand from the recent Core of Apparatus Genshin Impact event showcases this with his special machine that creates intricate mechanical toys. The region is governed by the Hydro Archon whose identity is yet unknown. Fontaine is based off real-world France.


Natlan– Even less is known about what to expect from Natlan, except that its central ideal is war, and it’s governed by the Pyro Archon, Murata. Venti (Barbatos) has described this Archon as “pretty obnoxious at times.” It is believed that Natlan could be based on Spanish or West African cultures.


Snezhnaya – Finally, there’s Snezhnaya, governed by the Cryo Archon, the Tsaritsa. This region has the strongest military force, from which the Fatui of Genshin Impact originates, although it’s unknown what the land’s guiding ideal is. Snezhnaya is reportedly a mix of influences from Russia and Italy.


Chasm - The Chasm is a subregion and an area located in The Chasm, Liyue. In the recent past, a series of mysterious accidents led the entire mine to be closed, putting its workers out of a job. However, the Liyue Qixing is gradually reopening the area, thus allowing miners to resume working. Beneath the Seven-Star Array is the Underground Mines.

Enkanomiya - Enkanomiya is the latest area to be added to Inazuma, and it sits under the region. You can only access Enkanomiya at first by jumping into the swirling pool in the middle of Watatsumi Island.

Khaenri’ah – Khaenri'ah is the infamous nation without a god. Kaeya of Genshin Impact, as well as Dainsleif, came from this land. The region faced a violent cataclysm at the hands of the gods, turning the original inhabitants into monsters that formed the Abyss Order. Now, they seek revenge.


Celestia – Celestia is a floating region high above Teyvat. It is believed to house the gods as well as mortals who have ascended to godhood.


Dark Sea – Finally, the Dark Sea is an umbrella term that refers to the lands beyond Teyvat. Inazuma borders the edge of the Dark Sea. It’s said that the gods who lost during the Genshin Impact Archon War fled to the Dark Sea, unwilling to live under the control of the official Archons.




Archon



War of Archons

A large-scale war that raged in Teyvat for several millennia and ended 2000 years before the start of the game. All the Gods of the world fought for the throne promised by Celestia (the abode of the Gods), and this event had an incredible number of participants. All the lands lay in ruins, and there was no rest for mere mortals anywhere, and no one could stay away. In each kingdom, the war proceeded in its own way, but the result was the same - found seven winners, the most powerful deities who created new kingdoms from the ruins of the former. After the end of the war, the era of the Seven Archons began, lasting until the present day. Of the original winners only two survived “Barbatos and Morax, the rest died for various reasons. Nevertheless part of the losers in the war survived and fled from the lands controlled by the victors, and now dwells outside the kingdoms. The place where they live now is called the "Dark Sea", and the apostates themselves are called "evil gods".

Barbatos-God of Freedom

Barbatos, God of freedom, and part-time Anemo Archon - the patron of Mondstadt. Initially, it was an ordinary wind elemental, a gentle breeze that lived among mortals and did not possess any special powers. He gained his divinity after defeating the former Anemo Archon - Decarabian, and absorbing his essence. This allowed him to take on the appearance of his best friend - a nameless bard who died in the war.In the same way, it was Barbatos who changed the relief and climate of Mondstadt, helped in the successful uprising and the creation of the Ordo Favonius, appointed the Guardians of the Four Winds ... and, with a completely honest look, somehow ceased to participate in the life of the region. At the moment he lives in the guise of a bard Venti, plays the lyre, has a clear addiction to alcohol and a mischievous character. He is one of the original Seven and is a playable character.


Morax-God of Contracts

Rex Lapis Morax, Lord of the Stone, God of contracts/ trade / history / war, the oldest of the Archons and the second living member of the original Seven. A Grand Adept whose true form is a large Chinese dragon with brownish-gold scales.Once a year, he appeared to the people of Li Yue at the Rite of Descension to give instructions. Created a common Teyvat currency - sea, the rules of trade, founded by Li Yue, convened those who protect the people from the demons Yax and Millelites. The Guyun Stone Forest is the cemetery of the Gods buried under the spears.Morax's alter ego in the mortal realm - Zhongli, a young consultant at Wangsheng Funeral Home who knows everything about everything. He honors contracts, is well versed in the history and traditions of Li Yue, dislikes seafood and Hydro Mimics, and in addition constantly forgets his wallet. Many acquaintances with him note that he looks young, but creates the feeling that he has lived for an eternity. Morax is over 6000 years old, so his long life has left an imprint on his personality. He is a playable hero and is famous for his incredible shields.


Raiden Shogun-God of Eternity

Raiden - Goddess of Thunder and Eternity, Electro Archon. The kingdom of Inazuma was ruled Twin Archons - Raiden Makoto and her Shadow Warrior, Raiden Eiwho won the war together. For all mortals, they are a single whole, but in fact Baal (the name of the Archon Makoto) and Beelzebub (the name of the Archon Ei) were engaged in different areas of activity. Baal was softer and preferred to deal with the people when Beelzebub fought in battles instead of her.Makoto died 500 years ago in a cataclysm, leaving only Hey, who became the new Archon, but the people did not notice the change. Beelzebub created puppet Raiden, leaving her to rule the people, and placed her soul in a sword. The traveler was able to reach the Archon and convey to her the dreams of the subjects.Now, after a long stay inside the Kingdom of Euthymia, she will once again recognize the world and trying to follow his ideal of eternityconsidering that change is inevitable. Ei relies on more than just her puppet's decisions, and is interested in modern life, though she's not too quick to pick up on trends. Also available as a playable character.


Buer-God of Knowledge

Lesser Ruler Kusanali, she is the Goddess of Wisdom - the current Dendro Archon, the youngest of the Seven, as she is only 500 years old. She had to take this place after the great sovereign Rukkhdevata began to eradicate forbidden knowledge from the region. Her ideal is wisdom, but as follows from the in-game information, wisdom itself is her enemy, and she can only humbly observe all the research at the Academy and the work of scientists, without having her own opinion.

Focalors-God of Justice

Goddess of Justice aka Hydro Archon - current ruler Fontaine. Her ideal is justice, and she longs to judge everyone, extolling the laws and the tribunal. Her court mania is confirmed dainsleif, which says that she loves the farce that is happening on them and is ready to judge even other Gods. The original inhabitants of Fontaine, the Oceanids who fled from this region, do not agree with her orders. It is also not included in the original Seven.Fontaine is the kingdom of the element Hydro, inspired by France and England. It invented the camera, rock and roll, Mona writes for Fontaine's Steam Bird magazine, suggesting that the country is highly technological and may have steampunk elements. Only one location in this region is known - the city of Petrikor, where unsurpassed clear waters (as follows from the description of the Dew of Renunciation, a drop from Hydro hypostasis). From the dialogue with Patrice, the event NPC, you can learn that the trial in Fontaine is worse than death.

Murata-God of War

Goddess of War, Lady of Fire, current Pyro Archon - Murata - is the mistress of the kingdom of Natlan. Zhongli and Venti speak about Murata, making it clear that they know her personally. Morax says: "The war rages like an unstoppable flame", and Barbatos: “She can be quite nasty at times. Pyro Archon is just a ferocious battle madness.". In ancient times, the tribes held rituals and holidays in her honor.Murata has own peopleblessed by her. They are called the children of Murata, or muratans, and their feature is fiery red hair, which is preserved when mixed with any blood. It was a hallmark of the tribe Mondstadt heroine Vennessa and herself.

Tsaritsa-The Queen

Goddess of an unknown ideal, Krio Archon, she is the Queen - ruler Snowy. She inherited her title from the previous Archon and is not part of the original Seven. Despite the fact that there was no meeting with her, one can encounter the activities of the Queen without problems - this Fatui, military-religious organization, implicitly obeying her, and her Harbingers. The followers of the Queen are distinguished by incredible devotion to her, and a vivid example of this is Tartaglia. At the moment, its main goal is collecting the Hearts of the Godsbelonging to other members of the Seven, and there is only one reason - she wants to declare war on Celestia.
